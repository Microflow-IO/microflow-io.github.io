# Why do we want to make Nano?

**Leo Young, 12/04/2024** 

Traffic analysis is a very traditional broad technology stack, covering many subdivision scenarios such as operation and maintenance monitoring and risk detection. There are a variety of programs and products, which seems to have little demand and innovation opportunities;

Is that really the case?

In fact, in the face of changes in software definition architecture and more and more sensitive computing resources, we are losing this huge and efficient technology stack capability;

- The hardware switch mirroring method under the traditional architecture is no longer applicable in the cloud environment due to the interference of the deployment location and HTTPS;
- Various SPANs based on the virtual switch have many blind spots, increase the burden of vSwitch, increase the bandwidth pressure, increase the computing cost of the server, and so on;
- The traffic analysis probe based on the local virtual machine is often not widely accepted because of large resource occupation, insufficient processing capacity, insufficient analysis granularity, and other reasons;
- The same problem also exists in the K8S cloud native environment.

This lack of ability, like the urban competent department, has lost the monitoring of the road network traffic flow, which has greatly affected the realization of many scenarios of high-quality operation and maintenance monitoring and risk detection.
